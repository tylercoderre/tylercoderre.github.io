Walsh Duffield

A New Web Presence for the Walsh Family

CHALLENGE

Walsh Duffield is a local insurance firm that was looking for a web presense for their family owned company. At 19 IDEAS we worked on a new website, creative work, and updated messaging to communicate their brand and values.

OUTCOME

My role involved working on the user experience and design of the website along with accompaning design work.

COMPLETED

  • User Experience
  • Web Design
  • Graphic Design

HOMEPAGE

For the homepage we wanted to communicate a brief overview of the Walsh Duffield Company's family values approach to their business. Being one of the oldest family owned companies in Buffalo and how they treat their employees and customers, we wanted to convey that to the pulic.

ABOUT

Through the homepage we dive deeper into Walsh's values and history, capping it off with a video (produced by 19 IDEAS) and an interactive timeline following tree rings.
